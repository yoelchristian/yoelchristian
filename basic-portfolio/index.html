About Me

Yoel Christian is an international student in University of California, Los Angeles. He came from Jakarta, Indonesia to Seattle in 2013 and attended Edmonds Community College at the age of 16. In 2016 after three years of completing high school diploma and a transfer degree, Yoel transferred to UCLA majoring in Actuarial Science aiming to be a professional actuary. However, after one year into the major, he felt that this line of work would not be suitable to his personality.


Yoel believes that working in the real world does not require hefty memorization of formulas and that being able to research and resolve problems with the use of available resources in the world wide web is more of an appreciated skill. With the respect for formal education, He decided not to drop out but investing very little time in his studies in UCLA with the goal of holding a degree.


In his young age, he believes that investing time into widening his knowledge is far more important than mastering a single line of profession. He has been travelling with his father in Indonesia as a salesman, working as a drum assembler, solving technical problems in the office, as well as visiting world trade cities in China.
